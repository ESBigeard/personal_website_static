I'm a PhD student in NLP working with medical data. My areas of work include medical domain, social media mining, drug non-compliance, drug misuse. People are not following their prescriptions and I want to find out why.

NLP or Natural Language Processing is an area of computer science concerned with processing natural language data, such as everything the doctors write in their patient's files and don't have time to organize properly. Or what people tell strangers on the internet but don't report to their doctors.
